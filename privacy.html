Privacy Policy for Kingdom-of-Chicken Bot

Last updated: June 2025

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Kingdom-of-Chicken Bot (“we,” “us,” “our”) collects, uses, and discloses information.

2. Information We Collect

  • Discord User ID: We store your Discord ID to keep track of “jailed” users and moderation logs.
  • Server Role Information: When a user is jailed, we back up their roles in a JSON file.

3. Use of Your Information

We use your Discord ID and role information only to implement the “jail” and “unjail” features and to log moderator actions. We do not share this data with third parties.

4. Data Retention

All logs and role backups remain until the file is manually deleted or the user is unjailed. We do not keep data longer than necessary for bot functionality.

5. User Rights

If you wish to have your data deleted (e.g., your role backup), contact us at your-email@example.com and we will remove your entries from our logs.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy at any time. Changes will be reflected here with an updated “Last updated” date.

7. Contact Us

For questions about this Privacy Policy, contact: your-email@example.com.
